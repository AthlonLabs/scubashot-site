Common questions

Why does my photo barely change?

ScubaShot corrects what it measures. A photo with little color cast or haze, like a bright shallow shot or a topside photo, gets a light touch on purpose. Raise Correction Strength or pick a preset if you want a stronger look.

How do the free exports work?

Every feature is free to use on as many photos as you like. The first 5 exports (saves or shares) are included; a single one-time purchase unlocks unlimited exports forever. No subscription.

I bought the unlock on another phone. How do I restore it?

Open the unlock screen and tap Restore Purchase while signed in to the same Apple Account. This works offline once restored.

Does ScubaShot upload my photos?

No. All correction runs on your device; photos never leave it. See the privacy policy.
