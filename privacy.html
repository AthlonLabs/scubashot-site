ScubaShot

Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

ScubaShot is built by AthlonLabs. The short version: your photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded, we have no accounts, no ads, and no tracking, and the only data that can leave your device is a small set of anonymous statistics that you agree to on first launch and can turn off at any time.

Your photos

All photo correction happens on your iPhone or iPad. Your photos, in original or edited form, are never transmitted to us or to anyone else by the app. Saving sends photos only to your own photo library; sharing goes only to the destination you pick in the share sheet.

Anonymous improvement data

On first launch, ScubaShot asks whether you want to share anonymous improvement data. If you agree, then each time you export a photo the app records: overall color and contrast statistics of the photo (a handful of numbers, not the image), the automatic correction values, your final slider settings, the export format, the batch size, your camera's make and model if the photo includes them, and the app version. This helps us tune the automatic correction for real underwater conditions.

This data contains no photos, no thumbnails, no location, no names, no device identifiers, and no account information. It is stored in Apple's CloudKit service in a database that is not linked to your Apple Account. You can turn sharing off at any time in Settings, and nothing is ever sent if you decline.

Location metadata in your exports

Photos from many cameras carry embedded location metadata. ScubaShot never reads or collects this itself; it only decides whether your exported files keep it. The "Include location in exported photos" toggle in Settings controls this. When it is off, ScubaShot strips GPS coordinates and location text from the files it exports.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. The app keeps a count of your free exports and your purchase state on your device and in your personal iCloud key-value storage so they survive reinstalls; this contains no personal information.

What we do not do

No accounts. No advertising. No analytics SDKs or third-party trackers. No selling or sharing of data with anyone. The app makes no network connections except Apple's StoreKit for purchases and, only with your consent, Apple's CloudKit for the anonymous statistics above.

Children

ScubaShot is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date.
